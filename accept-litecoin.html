Lower fees

Position Litecoin as another payment option that can cost less than traditional card processing.

How it works

Your BitPay-first launch path

Keep the first version simple. BitPay handles the processor side while LearnLitecoin handles setup, training, and support.

01

Collect merchant details

Business name, contact, website, location, type of checkout, and whether they want crypto or fiat settlement.

02

Create the BitPay account

Walk the owner through standard business signup, verification, and Litecoin enablement.

03

Set up the device

Use a tablet or phone, test the invoice flow, and make sure staff can create a sale and confirm payment.

04

Train the team

Leave them with a simple one-page cheat sheet and run a live test payment before launch.

What the merchant needs

  • A phone, tablet, or checkout screen
  • Internet connection
  • Business verification information
  • 30 to 60 minutes for setup

What LearnLitecoin provides

  • Merchant consultation
  • BitPay setup walkthrough
  • Litecoin enablement guidance
  • Staff training and test transaction
  • Launch checklist and support
