Merchant funnel

Connect merchants to Litecoin with BitPay first

For launch, keep the offer simple: LearnLitecoin helps businesses accept Litecoin and crypto using trusted processors, clear onboarding, and staff training.

01

Discovery call

Ask what kind of business they run, what checkout looks like, whether they want crypto or cash settlement, and if they want online, in-person, or both.

02

BitPay account setup

Walk them through creating a merchant account, verification, enabling Litecoin, and selecting settlement preferences.

03

POS configuration

Set up a tablet or phone checkout flow, test invoices, show the QR process, and train staff on payment confirmation.

04

Launch and support

Install signage, create a merchant cheat sheet, and provide follow-up support so they keep using it after day one.

Why a business would care

Lower fees. New customers. No chargebacks.

  • Accept Litecoin without building custom infrastructure.
  • Use BitPay for a familiar merchant experience and fiat settlement options.
  • Train staff with a simple QR-code checkout flow.
  • Position the business as forward-looking without overwhelming the owner.
