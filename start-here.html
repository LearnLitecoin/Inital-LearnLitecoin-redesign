Beginner guide

New to Litecoin? Start here.

LearnLitecoin is built to help normal people understand Litecoin in plain English and take the first step with confidence.

What is Litecoin?

Litecoin is a digital money network designed for fast, low-cost payments. It is open, global, and can be sent without a bank in the middle.

Why do people use it?

People use Litecoin because it is simple to send, inexpensive to use, widely listed, and built around payment utility instead of just speculation.
