Privacy education

MWEB explained without the nonsense.

LearnLitecoin can make MimbleWimble Extension Blocks one of the most useful parts of the site by teaching what it is, why it matters, and how to use it responsibly.

What it is

MWEB is Litecoin’s optional privacy extension that can improve confidentiality for amounts and transactions when supported by the wallet flow.

Why it matters

It gives users more choice around privacy and financial visibility without changing Litecoin’s core identity as a payments network.

How to use it

Show wallet support, teach the send-and-receive process, and explain that support can vary by service and exchange.
